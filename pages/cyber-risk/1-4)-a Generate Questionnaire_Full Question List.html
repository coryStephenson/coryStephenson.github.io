The questions will gauge how well you identify, protect, detect, respond to, and recover from cyber incidents.

Normally, you will interview each person face-to-face or by phone/video. If your organization is spread out over large distances or it’s too costly to conduct live interviews, send out the questionnaire using Survey Monkey, or similar. If your organization is very small, generating scores with a group of about six people is reasonable. Make one copy of this tab (or the following 3 tabs) for each interviewee. Put their name in cell H1.		*** This tab contains all the testable questions CRO created from the Framework.
However, you’ll want to only ask questions that are relevant to members of your organization.

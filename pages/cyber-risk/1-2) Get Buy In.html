An elevator speech is a clear, brief message designed to get "buy-in" for the Cyber Risk Management Action Plan you are going to build. It should communicate the approach and the benefits of the program. Your goal is to create buy-in at every level of your organization. Do a web search for "Create an Elevator Speech" for more info.The email below will help explain the purpose of the upcoming risk assessments. For maximum effect, see if an executive can send this email out company wide. This guarantees that employees will ready the email carefully and not be surprised when asked to participate in the interview process.Once employees have been chosen for an interview, send them the email below. The template explains the scope of the interview, how long the interview will take, and makes the purpose of interview clear. Most importantly, it reminds the interviewee that This is not a performance review.

SAMPLE: I am extremely concerned about our cybersecurity, and I hope you are too. The crooks are out there, and we can’t even begin to imagine the various ways they are penetrating companies and stealing trade secrets, money, customer lists, and disrupting businesses.

To combat this, we are implementing a Cyber Risk Management Action Plan that will begin by conducting interviews with key players in our organization. This isn't an audit. We will identify our top five cyber risks and learn how to balance our cybersecurity needs and still get our day-to-day business done on-time.

I'm reading a book called "Fire Doesn't Innovate" that is walking me through a very pragmatic approach to cybersecurity. I'm learning that cyber risk is not just a technology problem, it’s a management opportunity. Can I get you a copy to read?		RE: Cyber Risk Assessment

Hello team,

I am extremely concerned about our cybersecurity and hope you are too. The crooks are out there and we can’t even begin to imagine the various ways they are penetrating companies and stealing trade secrets, money, customer lists and generally disrupting businesses.

In order to combat this ongoing cyber issue, [Interviews Team] are assessing our cyber risks and creating a mitigation plan. We want to make sure you understand how this work will be done and how it will affect you and your team.

In order to understand the necessary elements of our operations, [Interviews Team] will interview select people in [month]. We need these interviews so we can best balance our security needs with our day to day business needs. Each interview will take 30-90 minutes and will be conducted [in-person] here in [city], if possible, or via [phone/Zoom] if not.

[Scheduler name] will be coordinating with each of you to find a workable time slot for your interview.

If you have any questions about the program, itself, let me know. If you have questions about the logistics of the interviews, let [Scheduler name] know.

Thank you!

[Executive Sponsor]		RE: Cyber Risk Assessment Interview

Hi [Name],

We are currently assessing our cyber risks.

You have been selected for an interview with [Interviewer Name]. We need these interviews so we can best balance our security needs with our day to day business needs.

Your interview will take about [45] minutes and will be conducted [explain how].

Our interview goal is to better understand our current cyber risk posture. We encourage you to be as candid as possible so our cyber risk mitigation plan will lower our risk while still letting you get your job done. In case you're wondering, we are not trying to evaluate your actions on the job with respect to cybersecurity.
